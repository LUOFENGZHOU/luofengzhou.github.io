About me: 

Welcome to my personal website! I am an incoming student in Master of Science in Financial Engineering (MSFE) at Columbia University. I have earned a B.A. in Economics, a B.S. in Mathematics and a B.Eng. in Computer Science from Wuhan University, China.

I am now a visiting student and research assistant at the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Jian Li. My research project is about the causal deep network and its application in financial markets. Before joining Tsinghua University, I was a reseach assistant at the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Bo Han. My works were mainly about causal inference and financial data mining.

My research interests fall into the intersection of Finance and Computer Science.

In my spare time, I usually play table tennis and listen to music. My favorite table tennis athlete is Joo Sae-Hyuk and my favorite singer is JJ Lin.
