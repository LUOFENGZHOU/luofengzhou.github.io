About me: 

Welcome to my personal website! I am a senior undergraduate in the Economics and Management School, Wuhan University, China. My major is Mathematical Finance and I also minor in Computer Science and Technology.

I was a research assistant in an interdisciplinary lab focusing on causal inference in macroeconomics, supervised by Prof. Bo Han and co-supervised by Prof. Ting Hu. After that I worked on private information in financial markets, supervised by Prof. Ting Hu. Now I am leading a research group focusing on high frequency causality in foreign exchange markets.

My research interests include causal inference, private information and market microstructure.

In my spare time, I usually play table tennis with one of my roommates. My favorite table tennis athlete is Joo Sae-Hyuk. Every month I go to the cinema with my girlfriend.
