About me: 

Welcome to my personal website! I am a second-year master student in Financial Engineering at Columbia University and a department research assistant at Columbia Business School, working with Prof. Simona Abis, Prof. Wei Jiang and Prof. Anton Lines. My current research interests include financial technology (FinTech) and empirical corporate finance.

Before joining Columbia, I earned a B.A. in Economics, a B.S. in Mathematics and a B.Eng. in Computer Science from Wuhan University, China.

During the summer of 2019, I was a visiting student and research assistant at the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University, hosted by Prof. Jian Li. My research project was mainly about the causal deep network and its application in financial markets. Before joining Tsinghua University, I was a reseach assistant at the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Bo Han. My works were mainly about causal inference and financial data mining.

In my spare time, I usually play table tennis and listen to music. My favorite table tennis athlete is Joo Sae-Hyuk and my favorite singer is JJ Lin.
